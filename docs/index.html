Privacy Policy for Pomodoro Timer

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

TL;DR: Our app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays completely on your device.

1. Data Collection

The Pomodoro Timer application is built with core principles of privacy and user autonomy. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics.

2. Local Storage

All of your settings, preferences, timer data, and statistics are stored locally on your device's internal storage. We have absolutely zero access to this information remotely, and it never leaves your physical phone.

3. Third-Party Services

This application operates entirely independently. It does not integrate with any kind of third-party tracking services, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or analytics tools that would compromise your privacy or collect your data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal data to share, sell, or disclose to any third parties, advertisers, or organizations. You are the sole custodian of your information.

5. Connectivity Permissions

Our app does not require internet permissions or network access to function correctly (beyond any platform-required baseline features). Your timer information stays offline.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we decide to iterate on our privacy policy due to new features, we will update this document accordingly. However, our commitment to zero data collection will remain foundational. Since we do not collect any user contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please open an issue publicly on our GitHub repository.
